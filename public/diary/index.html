FOREWORD#

I love reading (see notes from John Mike’s Library Gallery Notes), but also writing. This here is a collection of essays personally organized by me from 2016 to 2023. The motivation to write them comes nothing more than from the heat generated in the brain upon hearing, seeing, learning, and encountering some facts based on life. Not only did it give me a conscious thought, but it also drove me to write them down. Thus, it has become the text you are reading now. These words placed here are perhaps considered a bit of personal material. Moreover, perhaps they are a set of symbols that some people will never be able to understand and have absolutely no need to understand.
