These four long-term projects form one connected system. Each begins at a different layer, but all of them compound toward a single direction: answering the question, **“How to grow?”**

The Worker Investor begins with the future of an ordinary worker. Human Capital ETF asks what that person should invest in. Compounding Yourself examines how those investments become durable growth. Before the Genius studies how exceptional people developed such capabilities before their work became visible.

## Current Working Order

These are long-term experiments rather than a fixed roadmap. Some may be completed, reshaped, paused, or abandoned as my available time and interests change. The sequence is not guaranteed, but my current default working order is:

1. **The Worker Investor**
2. **Human Capital ETF**
3. **Before the Genius**
4. **Compounding Yourself**

This order reflects my current priorities, not a promise of sequence or completion.
